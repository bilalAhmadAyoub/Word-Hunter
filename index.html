HOW TO PLAY
Find a word (consisting of four or more characters) using the provided 16 letters and press enter to submit. A correct word increments your level by one and your score by the length of the word. Hint: there are always multiple words to find!
LEARN MORE
When a player types a valid word, the letters making up the word are selected. An English dictionary is iterated through to find the longest word that can fit into the game grid such that the word contains as many non-selected letters as possible. If the longest word is composed of letters not found among the non-selected letters, the selected letters will be replaced with new letters so that the longest word can fit in the game grid. Any unchanged selected letters are replaced with random letters, the level is incremented by one, and the score is incremented by the number of selected letters.
